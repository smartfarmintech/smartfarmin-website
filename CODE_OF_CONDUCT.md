# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Rythu360 is committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We are dedicated to creating an environment where all people feel safe and respected, regardless of their background, identity, experience, or perspective.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment:

- **Be Respectful:** Treat all community members with dignity and respect
- **Be Inclusive:** Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- **Be Collaborative:** Work together towards common goals
- **Be Professional:** Maintain professionalism in all communications
- **Be Patient:** Help others learn and grow
- **Be Constructive:** Provide helpful feedback and constructive criticism
- **Be Responsible:** Take responsibility for your words and actions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Discrimination based on identity (race, gender, sexuality, disability, etc.)
- Offensive, derogatory, or demeaning language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Threats or violent language
- Spam or self-promotion
- Sharing others' private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces (GitHub, Slack, Discord, forums)
- Project interactions and communications
- In-person events and conferences
- Professional conduct in community channels

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face:

- A warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from community spaces
- Other actions as deemed appropriate

### Reporting

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document the incident** with dates, times, and context
2. **Report to:** conduct@smartfarmintech.com
3. **Include:**
   - Your name and contact information
   - Description of the incident
   - Who was involved
   - Witnesses (if any)
   - Any relevant links or messages
   - The impact on you or others

All reports are confidential. We will investigate thoroughly and respond appropriately.

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct
- Taking appropriate action against violations
- Protecting the safety of all community members
- Providing a safe reporting process

## Consequences

### Level 1: Warning
- First-time minor violations
- Actions: Private warning, request for awareness

### Level 2: Temporary Suspension
- Repeated violations or severe behavior
- Actions: 1-7 day temporary removal from community spaces

### Level 3: Permanent Removal
- Severe, intentional, or repeated violations
- Actions: Permanent ban from community spaces

## Appeal Process

If you believe you've been unfairly sanctioned:

1. Submit an appeal to conduct@smartfarmintech.com within 7 days
2. Include details explaining why the decision was unfair
3. The appeal will be reviewed by a different maintainer
4. You'll receive a response within 14 days

## Examples

### Good Examples ✅

- "I disagree with your approach, but I appreciate you sharing your perspective"
- "Could you provide more context on why you chose that solution?"
- "Thanks for reporting this issue. Here's how we can fix it together"
- "Welcome! Feel free to ask questions as you're getting started"

### Bad Examples ❌

- "You're stupid, that solution will never work"
- "People like you don't belong in tech"
- "Stop commenting if you don't know what you're talking about"
- Sharing someone's personal information publicly
